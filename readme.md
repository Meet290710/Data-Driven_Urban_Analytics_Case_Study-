# ⚡ WattWatch: Smart City Energy Analytics

> 📊 A Data-Driven Urban Analytics Case Study using **SQL + Excel Dashboarding** to analyze smart city energy consumption and uncover sustainability insights.

---

## 🌆 Project Overview

**WattWatch** is an urban energy analytics project designed to simulate how municipal authorities can use data to monitor electricity consumption, detect inefficiencies, and optimize smart city infrastructure.

This project analyzes energy usage patterns across multiple city zones and consumer categories using:

- 🗄️ SQL for data analysis
- 📈 Excel Dashboard for visualization
- 📑 Data-driven insights for sustainable city planning

---

# 🚀 Objectives

✔ Analyze city-wide electricity consumption  
✔ Identify high-energy consumption zones  
✔ Detect outage-prone areas  
✔ Compare weekday vs weekend peak usage  
✔ Calculate estimated energy billing costs  
✔ Recommend smarter energy management strategies

# 🔍 Key Insights

## 📌 Findings

1. 🏭 Industrial consumers contribute the highest energy consumption across all zones.

2. 🌍 Central and South zones show the highest average power usage and outage frequency.

3. 📅 Weekend peak usage is slightly lower compared to weekdays.

4. ⚠ Faulty meters are concentrated in high-consumption areas.

5. 💰 Industrial-heavy zones show the highest estimated billing costs.

6. 🌱 East zone demonstrates the best energy efficiency.

7. 🛠 Predictive maintenance and smart scheduling can significantly reduce outages.
